### Problem Identified
Audited git log and prior session reports. The Group Attack system was added in two
previous sessions across three files:

The backend existed but players had no way to trigger group attacks from the menu.

### Fix Implemented
Added Group Attack submenu to `BattleCommandMenu.build_command_menu_items_with_targets()`:

**Menu Structure** (appears above Defer):
```
Group ▶
  ├── All-Out Attack   [disabled if any member has effective AP < 1]
  └── Limit Break      [disabled if any member has effective AP < 4]
```

**AP Check Logic**:
- `effective_ap = current_ap + 1` for the initiator (who already received natural +1 gain)
- `effective_ap = current_ap` for other members (haven't gained yet this turn)
- All-Out Attack: `effective_ap >= 1` per member — costs 1 AP each
- Limit Break: `effective_ap >= 4` per member — costs 4 AP each, 2× damage multiplier

**Selection Handler**: `_on_win98_menu_selection()` now routes `group_` item IDs to
`BattleManager.player_group_attack(group_type)`.

### Group Attack Damage Formula (recap)
```
base_damage = sum(attack_stats) × pow(1.5, N-1) × variance(0.85-1.15)
limit_break_damage = base_damage × 2.0
```
Examples at 20 attack per member:
- 2 members: 20+20=40 × 1.5 = **60**
- 3 members: 60 × 1.5 = **90**
- 4 members: 80 × 3.375 = **270** (Limit Break: **540** — six turns of natural AP to unlock)
